improve_accuracy(x, y, z)[source]¶

Create an estimate of the Z axis value using the X,Y axis values. This also improves the accuracy of the data. The Z-axis estimate gives a value for Z that makes the vector norm 1boldg (i.e. all three axes are perfectly orthogonal and X and Y are assumed to be correct). This estimate for Z can then be used to evaluate error that may correspond to pitch angle, or may be a constant error.

Parameters:
  • x – low pass filtered X axis accelerometer data
  • y – low pass filtered Y axis accelerometer data
  • z – low pass filtered Z axis accelerometer data
Returns:

A dictionary of accuracy dictionaries with keys: input_accuracy and improved_accuracy.
